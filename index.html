How it works: Total Privacy & Zero Latency

Unlike traditional editors that force you to upload sensitive documents to a cloud server, TezzPDF processes files entirely locally inside your browser using mathematical models. Your data never leaves your screen. This guarantees 100% privacy and eliminates upload wait times.
